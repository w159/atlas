# NinjaOne Ticket Sync

Invoke the `ninja-device-ticket-sync` skill with $ARGUMENTS. Resolve the NinjaOne device or alert, search ConnectWise for matching open work, and produce either the best ticket match or a ready-to-post ConnectWise ticket/update draft.
