# Minutes vs Granola AI

Last reviewed: 2026-04-09

## Quick verdict

- Choose **Granola AI** if you want a polished AI notepad, collaboration, and a product built around reading, editing, and sharing notes inside the app.
- Choose **Minutes** if you want local processing, inspectable files, and a memory layer that Claude, Codex, and other MCP clients can query later.

## Where Granola wins

- Polished standalone note-taking experience
- Better collaboration and integration story for teams
- Simpler feel for non-technical users who want one hosted app

## Where Minutes wins

- Local-first and file-native output
- Stronger developer / agent workflow depth
- Open source and inspectable markdown artifacts

## When Minutes is not the right fit

- When the top priority is a hosted collaborative note-taking product for teams
- When local files, inspectable output, and MCP workflows are not important
